Mountain climbing/hikes

Dominate the world from Africa's highest peaks, catch the first rays of sunrise to enjoy unique glaciers and watch the world shrink around you. Experience the majesty of these ancient rock formations and see them towering above the vast expanse of land that surrounds them, bringing heaven closer to you. African mountains are both easy and difficult to climb, making them popular with a wide range of experienced and novice climbers. The popular routes on Mount Kenya include Sirimon, which is a beginner-friendly, relatively straightforward climb with wildlife and distinctive foliage. Chogoria is the ideal safari-hiking destination because of its magnificent gorges, caverns, tarns, waterfalls, lakes, and animals. You can ascend Mount Kilimanjaro's Uhuru peak through the Machame, Lemosho, Umbwe, or Rongai routes. Depending on the route and individual preferences, lodging options include camps and cottages. We have equipment available for technical climbers to reach Mount Kenya's Nelion and Batian summits.
